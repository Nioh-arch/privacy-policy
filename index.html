Privacy Policy - Password Vault

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Password Vault does not collect, store, or share your personal data. All information stays locally on your device, is encrypted via AES-256-GCM, and is never transmitted to any server.The backup and import encryption keys are chosen by the user. This application uses the Google Advertising ID to display ads through the Google AdMob service. Donation transactions are handled entirely via Google Play Billing, and the app does not collect or store credit card numbers or payment details.
